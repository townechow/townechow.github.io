<p><code>Symbol.isConcatSpreadable</code> 用于配置某对象作为 <code>Array.prototype.concat()</code> 方法的参数时是否展开其数组元素。</p>
<pre class="hljs"><code class="language-js"><span class="hljs-keyword">let</span> s1 = [<span class="hljs-string">&#x27;c&#x27;</span>, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;d&#x27;</span>][(<span class="hljs-string">&#x27;a&#x27;</span>, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;b&#x27;</span>)].<span class="hljs-title function_">concat</span>(s1, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;e&#x27;</span>); <span class="hljs-comment">// [&#x27;a&#x27;, &#x27;b&#x27;, &#x27;c&#x27;, &#x27;d&#x27;, &#x27;e&#x27;]</span>

<span class="hljs-keyword">let</span> s2 = [<span class="hljs-string">&#x27;c&#x27;</span>, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;d&#x27;</span>];
s2[<span class="hljs-title class_">Symbol</span>.<span class="hljs-property">isConcatSpreadable</span>] = <span class="hljs-literal">false</span>[(<span class="hljs-string">&#x27;a&#x27;</span>, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;b&#x27;</span>)].<span class="hljs-title function_">concat</span>(s2, <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;e&#x27;</span>); <span class="hljs-comment">// [&#x27;a&#x27;, &#x27;b&#x27;, [&#x27;c&#x27;, &#x27;d&#x27;], &#x27;e&#x27;]</span>

<p>上面代码说明，数组的默认行为时可以展开，<code>Symbol.isConcatSpreadable</code> 默认等于 <code>undefined</code>。该属性等于 <code>ture</code> 时，也有展开的效果。</p>
<p>类似数组的对象正好相反，默认不展开。它的 <code>Symbol.isConcatSpreadable</code> 属性设为 <code>true</code>，才可以展开。</p>
