c:Te45,<h1>赋值运算符</h1>
<p>一个 <a href="https://www.ecma-international.org/ecma-262/9.0/index.html#sec-assignment-operators" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">赋值运算符</a>（assignment operator）将它右边操作数的值赋给它左边的操作数。</p>
<p>下列为 ECMAScript 标准规范的 Assignment Operator：</p>
